Project Overview

This web application is designed to serve as a comprehensive digital window for the village, bridging the gap between local potential and the wider public. It acts as a central hub for promoting edu-tourism, supporting local MSMEs (UMKM), and delivering official news.

Key Features & Modules

Edu-Tourism Management

A dedicated log of past study visits and field trips, complete with a public calendar of upcoming schedules to attract potential visitors.

Local Marketplace Catalog

A digital showcase for the village’s signature products (produk unggulan). It displays detailed items with real-time pricing and stock availability, boosting local economic visibility.

Data Synchronization

The system is integrated with Diskominfo, ensuring that all statistical data regarding the village is synchronized and accurate.

News & Updates

A dynamic portal to keep the community and the public informed about the latest village developments.

Backend & Administration: To ensure sustainability, the website is equipped with a robust Admin Dashboard. This feature empowers village officials to perform full CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations on all website content—from managing product inventories to publishing news—without requiring advanced technical skills.
